Old Paintings

Aug 12 2007, 4:53PM

I spent my entire undergrad and several years after that pretty seriously dedicated to canvas painting. There had always been a kind of formalism I was interested in that had to do with systematic arrangements and relationships. In early work this took the form as simplified narrative images with figures.

I'm posting a few paintings here that I did shortly before joining CADRE (2003-2004). At that point the formalism had turned into a logic of pattern and interlocking shapes. The interest that drove these now finds it's expression in code.
